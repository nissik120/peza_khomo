Privacy Policy & Licensing

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pezani Komo (HomeConnect) — a digital platform that connects tenants with property owners across Malawi. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information while ensuring transparent and fair use of our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal data that you voluntarily provide when creating an account or submitting property listings. This may include your name, phone number, email address, property details, and location information.

Additionally, we may collect technical data such as browser type, IP address, and usage statistics to help improve platform performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To connect property owners with verified tenants.
  • To facilitate communication between users through our platform.
  • To verify account authenticity and prevent fraudulent listings.
  • To improve user experience and platform reliability.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your information may only be shared with trusted service providers who help us operate the platform (e.g., hosting or payment processing), under strict confidentiality agreements.

All user data is encrypted in transit and stored securely in compliance with applicable data protection standards.

5. Licensing and Usage Rights

All content, designs, and code on this website are the intellectual property of HomeConnect unless otherwise stated. Unauthorized reproduction or redistribution of content without written permission is strictly prohibited.

Property listings and images uploaded by users remain their property, but by posting them on the platform, users grant HomeConnect a non-exclusive license to display, promote, and distribute such listings for marketing and operational purposes.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or delete their personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, please contact our support team at support@homeconnect.mw.
